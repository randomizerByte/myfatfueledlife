For too many years, dietary fat has been blamed for weight gain and poor health. Research continues to show this connection was completely backwards. In fact, fat is a high octane source of energy for our bodies and we need fat to be healthy. When we incorporate healthy fats into our diet while reducing carbohydrates, our bodies actually burn unwanted fat, provide endless energy, and help us feel amazing inside and out.

Low carbohydrate diets, such as the ketogenic diet are often done incorrectly. A lack of professional guidance can lead to an unsustainable and sometimes unhealthy outcome. There is no cookie-cutter approach to dieting and the ketogenic diet is no exception. My Fat Fueled Life focuses on diet diversity by tailoring a nutrition lifestyle that works for you.

About tara

...and her passion for fat fueled living

Tara Finnerty

Registered Dietitian Nutritionist

I'm a registered dietitian in Utah and owner of Sugar House Nutrition LLC. I provide nutritional support for people wanting to reap the many health benefits of the ketogenic (keto) diet. My passion for keto began working with children with severe epilepsy. Witnessing the incredible benefits for this population empowered my study for other potential benefits in health and disease. The keto diet has been criticized as being "unsustainable" and "impractical" however, I believe that with the right guidance the keto diet can become a healthy lifestyle option instead of another diet fad. While working with clients, I provide the support of a well-formulated and personalized diet plan that supports the nutritional diversity of each individual to provide healthy living that's achievable and sustainable!
