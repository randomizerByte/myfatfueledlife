The ketogenic (keto) diet has become the most popular diet of 2019 and continues to be widely studied and is gaining significant interest as a lifestyle tool to promote health and longevity. The keto diet offers a wide range of studied health benefits beyond weight loss which can include:

  • • Cardiovascular disease
  • • Cancer
  • • Diabetes
  • • Autoimmunity
  • • The adverse effects of aging
  • • Mitochondrial decline
  • • Obesity
  • • Chronic Pain
  • • Chronic infections
  • • Mood-related issues
  • • Epilepsy
  • • Neurological compromise, decline and dementia/Alzheimerâ€™s

In addition, the diet has gained interest for many endurance athletes, as well as for those who simply just want to feel more energetic with goals of increasing health and life span.

Unfortunately, due to the rise in popularity of keto, there is a significant amount of misinformation, which results in lack of sustainability, poor outcomes, and concern for overall safety.

By the end of the workshop, attendees will learn and appreciate the following:

  • • The enormous range of benefits that come with an effective state of ketogenic adaptation
  • • What critical steps are needed in order to successfully adopt and maintain this dietary approach
  • • What foods can help optimize the most effective state of ketosis possible, and what foods are likelier to interfere
  • • Why some ketogenic diets fail

Come learn how keto is unlike any other diet that has ever existed and the critical steps needed to turn it into a healthy way-of-eating, instead of just another failed attempt at losing weight.

The workshop is lead by Tara Finnerty RDN, CD, a registered and licensed clinical dietitian and ketogenic specialist with 6 years of clinical ketogenic experience. Tara is a regular contirbutor to hip2keto.com and has been featured on KSL5, ABC, and Fox13.

The workshop will be 4 hours long over two consecutive Saturdays lasting 2 hours each. In addition, participants will receive a complete ketogenic guide with all the tools and tips to help you on your journey.
