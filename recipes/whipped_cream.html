Fat-Fueled Whipped Creme

Makes ~ 8 ounces

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract (or flavor of choice)
  • ¼ cup Swerve confectioners’ sugar

Instructions:

  1. Using a hand mixer, whip heavy whipping cream until stiff peaks form
  2. Mix in extract and mix till full incorporated
  3. Add Swerve and fold in using a spatula until well mixed.
