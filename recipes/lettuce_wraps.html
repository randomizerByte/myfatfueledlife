Thai Turkey Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 Green Onions
  • 1 Head of Butter Lettuce
  • 12oz. Ground Turkey
  • 2 fl. oz. Hoison Sauce
  • 3 oz. Water Chestnuts
  • 2 tsp. Sriacha
  • 1 tsp. Chopped Ginger
  • 4 oz. Slaw Mix
  • 1 1/2 fl. oz. Asian Sesame Dressing

Instructions:

  1. Trim and thinly slice green onions on an angle, keeping white and green portions separate. Separate leaves of lettuce for cups.
  2. Place a medium non-stick pan over medium-high heat with 2 tsp. olive oil. Add ground turkey to hot pan. Stir often, breaking up with a spoon, until no pink remains, 8-10 minutes.
  3. Add hoisin sauce, water chestnuts, half the Sriracha, white portions of green onions, and ginger to pan. Stir occasionally, 3-4 minutes. Taste, and add remaining Sriracha and a pinch of salt and pepper, if desired. Remove from burner.
  4. Place a lettuce cup on a clean work surface (or layer two cups for extra crunch and structural support). Fill with a few spoonfuls of turkey mixture and top with slaw mix. Repeat for about five more lettuce cups.
