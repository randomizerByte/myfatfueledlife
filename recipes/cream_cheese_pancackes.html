Fat-Fueled Pancakes

Makes ~ 16-18 small 4-inch pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 ounces full-fat cream cheese
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 packets of stevia or (~6 drops of liquid stevia)
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until fully incorporated and smooth.
  2. Heat a large skillet to ~350 °F or medium-high heat if using stove-top.
  3. Pour ~ 1/8th cup or 2 Tablespoons of batter per pancake onto pre-heated skillet.
  4. Flip pancake when bubbles start to appear and sides of pancake are set.
  5. Add your favorite topping and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts:

Serving Size: 4 (4-inch) pancakes
Calories: 172
Net Carbs: 2 gm
Fat: 15 gm
Protein: 10 gm

Optional Toppings:

  • Fat-Fueled Whipped Creme
  • Melted Butter
  • Berries
