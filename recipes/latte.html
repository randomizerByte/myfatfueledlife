Fat-Fueled Mocha

A great way to fuel up for the day!

Ingredients:

  • 14-ounce fresh brewed hot coffee (can replace with a hot tea or heated unsweetened vanilla almond or coconut milk)
  • 1 Tbsp of butter (I prefer grass-fed European butter—higher fat)
  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil or pure MCT oil
  • 1 Tbsp 100% unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4-6 drops of liquid stevia
  • ¼ tsp pure vanilla extract

Add-in Options:

  • 1 Tbsp keto-friendly protein powder (any flavor, I prefer chocolate)
  • 1 Tbsp heavy cream (use as a replacement for the coconut oil or the butter)
  • 1 Tbsp creamy nut butter
  • Dash of nutmeg

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients except the cinnamon to a blender and blend on high for ~45 seconds-1 minute.
  2. Pour into a 16-20 ounce cup and sprinkle a dash of cinnamon on top.
  3. Enjoy!
